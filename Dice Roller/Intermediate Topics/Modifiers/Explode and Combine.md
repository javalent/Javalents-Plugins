## Explode and Combine

**Syntax**: `XdX!!{n|i}`

Equivalent to explode, but exploded dice are combined in the tooltip display.

### Examples

| Formula        | Result          |
| -------------- | --------------- |
| `dice: 2d20!!` | `[7, 28!] = 34` |
| `dice: 2d4!!3` | `[3, 10!] = 13` |
| `dice: 1d1!!i` | `[100!] = 100`  |
