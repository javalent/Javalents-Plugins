**Syntax**: `Xd[Y, Z]`

Create a custom die, with a minimum of Y and a maximum of Z.

## Example

| Formula            | Result         |
| ------------------ | -------------- |
| `dice: 4d[7, 8]`   | `[7, 7, 8, 7]` |
| `dice: 1d[20, 20]` | `[20]`         |
